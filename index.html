  • Case study: Data.gov.uk

    The UK Government’s official open data portal provides a central “way into the wealth of government data” and aims to make that data “easy to find; easy to license; and easy to re-use.”

  • Case study: PublicData.eu

    PublicData.eu is a research prototype of a pan-European data catalogue and federation mechanism, running on CKAN and developed as part of the FP7-funded LOD2 project.

  • Case study: Helsinki

    The Helsinki Region Infoshare project aims to make regional information quickly and easily accessible to all. It is powered by CKAN and WordPress, using CKAN for the core functionality.

  • Case study: International Aid Transparency Initiative

    The International Aid Transparency Initiative is about making aid spending information easier to access and understand. It provides a common standard for funding organizations.
